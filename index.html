Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix the milk
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Add in the flour and mix untilcombined into dough
  4. knead the dough
  5. roll and large rectangles
  6. melt the butter for filling
